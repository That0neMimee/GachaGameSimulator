Gacha Game Simulator

Welcome to DragonMarquise's alpha-prototype gacha simulator!

To use this simulator, go to the Gacha Currency Generator page to get Currency. Then go to the different pull pages to
spend the Currecny on pulls. Also, the gallery allows you to view individual characters without needing to do pulls,
and the history page lets you check what you got on past pulls, up to 50 previous pulls.

Hope you have fun with this simulator!
