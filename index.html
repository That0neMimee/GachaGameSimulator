Gacha Game Simulator

Welcome to DragonMarquise's alpha-prototype gacha simulator!

To use this simulator, go to the Gacha Zenny Generator page to get Zenny. Then go to the different pull pages to
spend the Zenny on pulls. Also, the gallery allows you to view individual characters without needing to do pulls,
and the history page lets you check what you got on past pulls, up to 50 previous pulls.

Hope you have fun with this simulator!

"True Gacha Mode" is where the rates in this simulator are similar to actual gacha games. That is, they will be far
more brutal and unforgiving. You can enable or disable True Gacha Mode using the button below.
